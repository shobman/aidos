# Issues Log

Centralised register of escalated issues and decisions across the project.

Items are copied here from individual artifacts when they need visibility beyond the artifact they originated in — typically when they're escalated for stakeholder decision or when they affect multiple workstreams.

This log does not replace inline issue tracking in artifacts. It surfaces what's waiting for a decision and what's been decided, so nothing gets lost in meeting notes and Slack threads.
